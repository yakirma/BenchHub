All leaderboards

Each model's results are combined across every board in a category into one score. Per board, scores are normalized to 0–1 (best = 1, worst = 0, respecting higher- vs lower-is-better); a model's category score is the mean of those. Coverage shows how many of the category's boards a model has been run on — all models are ranked, so weigh a high score against its coverage.
