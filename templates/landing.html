How it works

Three steps from idea to ranked leaderboard.

1. Pick a dataset

Upload a ZIP of your ground-truth samples or pick one from the public catalog. Folder conventions auto-detect images, scalars, histograms, and depth maps.

2. Define metrics in Python

Bring your own scoring code — any function that takes ground truth + prediction and returns a number. Per-sample or aggregated. No DSL to learn.

3. Submit, get ranked

Upload a predictions ZIP, BenchHub runs your metrics in a sandbox, and your run lands on the leaderboard next to everyone else's.
